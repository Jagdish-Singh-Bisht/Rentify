Owner Support & FAQs

How do I list a property?

You can list a property by visiting owner-add.php and filling out the property details form.

Can I edit or delete my listings?

Yes, you can manage your listings via owner-listings.php where options to edit or remove properties will be available.

Is there any listing fee?

No, Rentify currently offers free property listings for owners.

How will I be contacted by interested tenants?

Tenants can view your contact details once they are logged in. Make sure to keep your profile and contact information updated.
